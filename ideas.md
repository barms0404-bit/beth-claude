# Armstrong Arikat Research Terminal - Design Brainstorm

## Context
An institutional-grade equity research dashboard for Armstrong Arikat Private Wealth Group. The interface needs dropdown navigation and push buttons to all report sections. The branding is gold/black with a luxurious, high-finance aesthetic.

## Idea 1: "Bloomberg Terminal Noir"

**Design Movement:** Dark Financial Terminal meets Art Deco luxury
**Core Principles:** Information density without clutter, gold-on-black contrast hierarchy, institutional gravitas, data-first layout
**Color Philosophy:** Pure black (#000000) as the canvas representing authority and sophistication. Gold (#C9A961) as the singular accent color conveying wealth and exclusivity. Cream (#F5E6C8) for readable body text. Red/Green only for market direction indicators.
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width horizontal toolbar at top with evenly-spaced navigation buttons and dropdowns. Below, a continuous scrolling content area with card-based sections. No sidebar — all navigation lives in the top bar. Logo watermark centered behind content at 6% opacity.
**Signature Elements:** Thin gold horizontal rules separating sections. Subtle gold glow on hover states. The AA monogram as a large ghosted watermark behind all content.
**Interaction Philosophy:** Instant, no-nonsense interactions. Buttons respond with crisp scale transforms. Dropdowns appear without delay. Scrolling is smooth but not bouncy.
**Animation:** Minimal and purposeful. Section cards fade in on scroll with 150ms transitions. Button press scales to 0.97 in 100ms. Dropdown menus slide down in 180ms with ease-out. No decorative animations.
**Typography System:** Cormorant Garamond for headings (weight 500-600, letter-spacing 2px). Inter for body text and data (weight 300-500). Monospace for ticker symbols and numerical data.

## Idea 2: "Gilded Manuscript"

**Design Movement:** Renaissance manuscript meets modern data visualization
**Core Principles:** Scholarly authority, illuminated manuscript aesthetics with modern functionality, layered information hierarchy, ceremonial presentation of data
**Color Philosophy:** Deep obsidian (#0A0A0A) as parchment-dark background. Gold (#C9A961) used like illuminated manuscript gilding — for initial capitals, borders, and key data points. Warm cream (#F5E6C8) as the primary reading color evoking aged vellum.
**Layout Paradigm:** Top navigation bar styled as a decorative header band with ornamental gold borders. Content flows in a single-column manuscript style with wide margins. Sections are separated by decorative gold dividers. Cards have subtle inner borders reminiscent of manuscript frames.
**Signature Elements:** Decorative gold corner flourishes on key cards. Section headers styled like chapter openings with oversized Cormorant Garamond initials. Subtle paper-grain texture overlay at 3% opacity.
**Interaction Philosophy:** Deliberate and weighty. Buttons have a slight delay before responding, conveying importance. Hover states reveal additional gold ornamentation.
**Animation:** Stately entrance animations — content fades up from below with 250ms timing. Gold borders draw themselves in on scroll. Dropdown menus unfurl like scrolls (200ms ease-out).
**Typography System:** Cormorant Garamond at display sizes (36-48px) for section headers with generous letter-spacing (6-8px). Inter Light (300) for body text at 15-16px. Small caps for labels and metadata.

## Idea 3: "Sovereign Command Center"

**Design Movement:** Military command center meets private banking interface
**Core Principles:** Operational clarity, hierarchical information flow, zero ambiguity, decisive action-oriented design
**Color Philosophy:** Absolute black (#000000) as the void — representing the seriousness of capital deployment. Gold (#C9A961) exclusively for actionable elements and critical data. Muted gold (#8A7548) for secondary information. No decorative use of color — every gold element means "pay attention here."
**Layout Paradigm:** Fixed top command bar with large, clearly-labeled push buttons and dropdown selectors — styled like instrument panel controls. Content area below uses a grid of status cards that can be scanned quickly. Each section is a self-contained "briefing panel" with clear headers and dense data.
**Signature Elements:** Thick gold top-border on the navigation bar (4px). Cards with left-side gold accent bars indicating priority. Status indicators using filled/hollow gold circles. The AA logo rendered as a large subtle watermark.
**Interaction Philosophy:** Military precision — every click produces immediate, visible feedback. Buttons have distinct active states (gold fill on press). No ambiguity about what's clickable.
**Animation:** Crisp and functional. Cards enter with a 120ms scale-up from 0.98. Buttons flash gold on click (80ms). Section transitions are instant cuts, not fades. Data updates pulse once in gold then settle.
**Typography System:** Inter Semi-Bold (600) for navigation buttons at 13px with letter-spacing 1px (all-caps). Cormorant Garamond Medium (500) for section titles at 28-32px. Inter Regular (400) for data at 14px. Tabular numbers for all financial figures.

## Selected Approach: Idea 3 — "Sovereign Command Center"

This approach best matches the user's requirements for:
- Push buttons and dropdowns prominently in the top navigation
- Professional, institutional-grade feel
- Action-oriented interface with clear navigation
- Gold/black branding with the logo as background watermark
- No hero page — continuous flow of content across all sections
